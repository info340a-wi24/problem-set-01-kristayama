SNOOPY

Snoopy is a dog from the Peanuts Franchise which consists of comic strips, multiple tv shows, movies, and more. Snoopy's breed is a beagle and is known to have an angsty and funny personality. His owner is Charlie Brown and he is often seen hanging out with Woodstock, a yellow bird. Snoopy lives in a doghouse, but he is frequently seen layng atop the roof, rather than inside.

Reasons why I admire and love Snoopy:

  • He is very cute in appearance.
  • I think it's funny that he sleeps on top of his house.
  • His sometimes sassy and indignant personality is relatable!
